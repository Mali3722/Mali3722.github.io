Senior Missionary services

We offer long term stay for missionary couples assigned to work in temple. Our two bedroom units offer all the comforts of home. Each unit has a kitchen, laundry and is furnished. Also, when you stay with us you have access to all of the ammenities our hotel offers. We also provide 10% discounts for any family members that may come to visit.
